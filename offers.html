Why choose CreditLk

Finding the right loan in Sri Lanka should be simple and transparent. With CreditLk you can compare offers from multiple licensed lenders in one place and submit your application online in just a few steps — no queues, no confusing paperwork.

  • Clear comparison: see rates, fees and repayment terms side-by-side before you apply.
  • Intro offers (when available): some lenders provide first-loan promotions (including 0% offers for eligible borrowers).
  • No platform fees: using CreditLk is free for you.
  • Trusted partners: we work only with lenders authorized to operate in Sri Lanka.
  • Fast online process: complete an online form; the lender will review your application.

How we work: CreditLk is a loan comparison service — not a lender. We help you compare options and choose what fits your budget. Loans are issued by the bank/lender you select, and final approval, rates and fees are determined by that lender after reviewing your application.

Responsible borrowing: Borrow only what you can repay. Late or missed payments may result in additional charges and could affect your credit history.
