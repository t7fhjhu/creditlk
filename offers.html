Loan Information

CreditLK provides independent information about loan offers. We are not a lender and do not make credit decisions. All terms and final decisions are made directly by the lender.

Key Terms

  • Loan term: from 65 to 360 days
  • Maximum Annual Percentage Rate (APR): 24%
  • Example: A loan of €1,000 for 90 days at 24% APR → estimated interest is approximately €59.18; total repayment is €1,059.18.

Additional Information

  • Eligibility: 18+, Sri Lankan NIC, and an active bank account.
  • Approval & funding: Same-day in many cases; timing depends on the lender and bank cut-off times.
  • Credit checks: We may use a soft check for matching; lenders may run a hard check before disbursing.
  • Early repayment: Usually allowed; some lenders reduce interest if you repay early.
  • Late fees/penalties: Determined by each lender; paying late increases total cost.
  • Responsible borrowing: Borrow only what you can repay on time.

Important Information

Before applying for a loan, carefully review the terms provided by the specific lender. Check the repayment schedule, interest rate, and any applicable fees. Do not take a loan if you are unsure about your ability to repay it on time.
